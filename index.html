Evolution of Performance.

RED SPIKE wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born on the track, during the final 100 meters where every millisecond counts. We realized that standard footwear was leaking energy—energy that athletes were working hard to generate.

Our mission is simple: **Zero Leakage.** We engineer footwear that acts as an extension of your biomechanics. Through hundreds of iterations and elite testing in Chandigarh, we've developed the Spike Architecture.

Beyond The Fabric

Acoustic Engineering

We study the sound of a footstrike. A RED SPIKE strike is tuned to produce a specific low-frequency resonance that indicates perfect energy transfer. If it doesn't sound right, it's not a RED SPIKE.

Chemical Bonding

We use solvent-free, water-based adhesives that form a molecular bond between the upper and the sole, ensuring the shoe never separates under high-heat competition environments.
